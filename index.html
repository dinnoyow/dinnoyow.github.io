About me.

I'm an artist and developer interested in creating applications and games that have value. I'm inspired by and aspire to example Valve's philosophy at work.

I'm Indonesian, grew up in Indonesia, and I've finished my high school degree. I'm currently gaining more skills in art, security, and programming.

Besides academic studies, I've been self-taught in the pursuit of my interests, which gives me an important insight: There's always a way to make sense of anything, and what you need is to find your footing. Until you find it, curiosity is your teacher.

I love music, manga, motion pictures, and video games. My main goal is to create works that will leave marks on people's minds.

@dinnoyow is a nickname I use for my internet-related activities and account usernames. It is used for anonymity.
